About David Cylex

David Cylex writes fierce, fast-paced fantasy where misfits bond with monsters—and become something more. He is the author of Arcbeasts, a sweeping sci-fi fantasy series about psychic creatures, secret legacies, and the power of choosing who you become.

His books are known for cinematic action, emotionally driven heroes, and world-shattering stakes wrapped in vivid, lyrical prose. When he’s not plotting Arcbeast battles or teasing rival-bonded romance, he’s probably overthinking plot twists and wondering if his favorite character survived the chapter.

David lives between futures—one full of technology, one full of magic—and he writes for the readers who do too.
